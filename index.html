Directions to KoolKats

The nearest airport to get to koolkats is Kansas City International Airport. (MCI)
    There are 3 ways to get to Maryville, MO from MCI
  1. Car
  2. Bus
  3. Uber
    1. If you are driving, Start at the Airport NW 120th Street
    2. Get on I-29 N and Drive for 43.5 miles
    3. Then take the exit US-71 and drive for 32.8 more miles
    4. You'll reach E 2nd St in Maryville
    5. Kool Kats will be on your right hand side.
